Create this demo for the APP named Sharelot and the demo is designed for this first customer Regrow Group

1. Purpose
* Helps a business (Regrow Group) manage and deploy review-sharing campaigns.
* Customers can select AI-generated reviews and before/after photos, then post them to social platforms via QR codes.
* Owners can track QR code scans, optimize campaigns, and download print-ready assets.

2. Main Features
Customer Flow:
1. Drafting Page
    * Select a review snippet (AI-generated) and a photo.
    * Continue to platform selection once both are selected.
2. Platform Selection
    * Choose where to post the review: Google, Facebook, Instagram, XiaoHongShu.
    * Clicking a platform copies the review content to the clipboard.
    * Simulated redirection or QR scan workflow.
3. AI Optimization
    * Tracks total clicks per platform.
    * Generates recommendations based on click-through rates.

Owner/Admin Flow:
1. Dashboard
    * View all QR codes for different placement types (Counter, Table Tent, Receipt, Business Card).
    * Track total scans and highest performing platforms.
    * AI-generated summary and recommendations.
    * External review URL finder with AI suggestions.
    * Download traffic reports and print-ready PDF kits.

3. Other Functionalities
* Language toggle (English/Chinese) with translations for all text.
* QR code approval modal for verifying links before deployment.
* Persistent state saved in localStorage:
    * Selected review/photo
    * QR links
    * Click statistics
* Platform detection: recognizes URLs for supported platforms automatically.
* Styling: TailwindCSS and custom CSS for cards, modals, grids, buttons, and animations.

In short, this is a multi-view interactive web app that lets businesses generate, deploy, track, and optimize review-sharing campaigns via QR codes, with an AI-assisted recommendation system and multi-language support.
