## Before You Start

### Browser Settings
- **Allow Pop-ups**: Some features open social media apps or websites in new windows. Make sure your browser allows pop-ups from this site.
- **Best Browsers**: Chrome, Safari, or Firefox work best.
- **Mobile Devices**: For the best experience on phones, use the device's default browser.

### What You'll Need
- Your business social media URLs (Facebook, Instagram, XiaoHongShu, etc.)
- Photos of your shop or products
- Access to your Admin Dashboard

## Shop View - Customer Experience

The Shop View is what your customers see when they visit your ShareLor page. It's designed to make sharing reviews quick and easy.

### Main Screen Layout

![Shop View Main Screen](../attached_assets/help/shop-view-main.png)
*Screenshot: The Shop View shows a photo slider on the left and platform buttons on the right*

**What customers see:**
1. **Photo Slider** - Your shop photos rotate automatically
2. **Business Name** - Displayed at the top of the button area
3. **Platform Buttons** - Customers tap these to share reviews
4. **QR Code** - Links to the Quick View for mobile access

### Platform Buttons

Your customers can choose from these platforms:

| Button | What It Does |
|--------|-------------|
| **Google Reviews** | Opens the review drafting flow for Google |
| **XiaoHongShu** | Opens review drafting for XiaoHongShu (小红书) |
| **Instagram** | Opens review drafting for Instagram posts |
| **Facebook** | Opens review drafting for Facebook posts |
| **Follow Facebook** | Opens your Facebook page directly |
| **Follow Instagram** | Opens your Instagram profile directly |

### Follow Facebook Button

![Follow Facebook Modal](../attached_assets/help/follow-facebook-modal.png)
*Screenshot: The Follow Facebook modal with your business name and a single button*

When customers tap "Follow Facebook":
1. A popup appears with your business name
2. They tap "Follow on Facebook"
3. Your Facebook page opens
4. They tap Follow on Facebook

### Follow Instagram Button

![Follow Instagram Modal](../attached_assets/help/follow-instagram-modal.png)
*Screenshot: The Follow Instagram modal with gradient styling*

When customers tap "Follow Instagram":
1. A popup appears with your business name
2. They tap "Follow on Instagram"
3. Your Instagram profile opens (opens the app on mobile!)
4. They tap Follow on Instagram

### XiaoHongShu Button

![XiaoHongShu Modal](../attached_assets/help/xhs-modal.png)
*Screenshot: The XiaoHongShu modal showing the review text preview*

When customers tap "XiaoHongShu":
1. A popup shows a preview of the review text
2. They tap "Copy & Open XiaoHongShu"
3. The text is copied to their clipboard
4. XiaoHongShu app opens (or website on desktop)
5. They paste the text and post!

## Quick View - Mobile Experience

The Quick View is a streamlined mobile version accessed by scanning the QR code.

### Accessing Quick View

![QR Code Section](../attached_assets/help/qr-code-section.png)

## Admin Dashboard

The Admin Dashboard is where you manage your ShareLor settings.

### Accessing the Dashboard

Go to: `yoursite.com/admin`

![Admin Dashboard](../attached_assets/help/admin-dashboard.png)
*Screenshot: The Admin Dashboard main view*

### Configuration Tab

#### Business Information
- **Website URL**: Your business website (used for AI to discover social links)
- **Google Place ID**: Links to your Google Business for reviews

#### Social Media URLs
Set up links for each platform:
- Google Reviews URL
- Facebook Page URL
- Instagram Profile URL
- XiaoHongShu Profile URL
- TikTok Profile URL
- WhatsApp URL

**Tip**: Only platforms with URLs configured will show buttons in Shop View!

#### Shop Photos
- Upload photos that customers can choose when sharing
- These appear in both the photo slider and drafting flow

#### Slider Photos
- Upload photos specifically for the Shop View photo slider
- These rotate automatically every 4 seconds

#### Review Hashtags
- Add hashtags that customers can include in their posts
- AI can suggest hashtags based on your website

### Analytics Tab

![Analytics Dashboard](../attached_assets/help/admin-analytics.png)
*Screenshot: The Analytics view showing click counts*

Track how many times each platform button is clicked:
- See which platforms are most popular
- Monitor your campaign performance
- Make data-driven decisions

### Print Materials Tab

Generate QR codes and print-ready materials:
- Download QR codes for your Quick View
- Get table tent templates
- Print counter signs

## Troubleshooting

### "Nothing happens when I click a button"

**Solution**: Check your browser's pop-up blocker
1. Look for a blocked pop-up icon in your browser's address bar
2. Click it and choose "Always allow pop-ups from this site"
3. Try the button again

### "Platform button is not showing"

**Solution**: The URL is not configured
1. Go to Admin Dashboard
2. Check that the platform URL is filled in
3. Click "Save Changes"

### "XiaoHongShu doesn't open the app"

**Solution**: The app may not be installed
- On mobile: Install the XiaoHongShu app from the App Store or Play Store
- On desktop: The website opens instead (normal behavior)

### "Instagram opens the website instead of the app"

**Solution**: This can happen if:
- The Instagram app isn't installed
- You're on a desktop computer
- The deep link wasn't recognized

**Workaround**: The website works the same way - just log in and follow!

### "My photos aren't showing"

**Solution**: Check photo uploads
1. Go to Admin Dashboard
2. Verify photos are uploaded in Shop Photos or Slider Photos
3. Wait a moment for them to load
4. Refresh the Shop View page
